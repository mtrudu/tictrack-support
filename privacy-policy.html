1. Introduction

MT's Apps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the TicTrack Counter mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Data Collection

TicTrack Counter does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All your counters, settings, and data are stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage).

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All data remains on your device
  • No Cloud Backup: We don't sync or backup your data
  • No Account Required: No registration or login needed
  • No Analytics: We don't track your usage patterns

4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send milestone and daily reminders (optional)
  • Local Storage: To save your counters on your device

5. Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability.

Data collected: Technical crash information only (no personal data)

Purpose: Fix bugs and improve app performance

Data sharing: Anonymous technical data only with Google Firebase

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting (Google)
  • Local Notifications: Device-only reminders

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you control your data security through your device's security settings.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
